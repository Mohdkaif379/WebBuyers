Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about projects, pricing, delivery, and post-purchase support.

What type of projects do you provide?

We provide a wide range of projects including business websites, e-commerce stores, admin dashboards, portfolio sites, college final-year submissions, and custom software solutions. Our projects are designed for students, startups, and business owners who need practical and ready-to-use solutions. We can also create fully custom projects based on your feature list, preferred technology stack, and budget.

Do I get source code with my purchase?

Yes, you receive full source code with all required files and assets. Along with code, we also provide setup guidance so you can run the project without confusion. In most cases, you will get clear structure for frontend and backend files, helping you easily edit content, update branding, and continue development when needed.

How long does delivery take?

Ready-made projects are usually delivered instantly or within a few hours after confirmation. For custom projects, delivery depends on scope, design complexity, integrations, and revision count. Small custom work can often be completed in 1 to 3 days, while larger business systems may require more time. We always share an estimated timeline before starting so expectations stay clear.

Do you provide customization?

Yes, we provide full customization for UI design, features, pages, workflows, and integrations. You can request changes like custom forms, admin roles, payment options, analytics sections, API connections, and specific business logic. Before development starts, we review requirements and share the final plan with cost and timeline so you know exactly what is included.

Is support available after delivery?

Yes, we provide post-delivery support for setup help, deployment guidance, and basic troubleshooting. If you face issues while running the project, our team assists you with practical steps to make everything work correctly. For long-term maintenance, feature upgrades, or advanced support, you can also opt for extended service based on your project needs.

How can I contact your team quickly?

You can contact us quickly using the Contact page form by sharing your service name, requirement details, expected timeline, and budget. This helps us respond faster with an accurate plan. You can also email us directly if you prefer one-to-one communication. For fastest support, include references or sample links so we can understand your exact requirement in the first message.
